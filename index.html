Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

Range of Motion is how far a joint can move through its full arc of motion. In BJJ, limited ROM at the hip, shoulder, ankle, or spine can prevent you from achieving proper technique positions, and increases injury risk. ROMRxBJJ™ measures 14 joints bilaterally to build a complete picture of your mobility.
You’ll use a free goniometer app on your smartphone to measure joint angles at home. Each measurement takes seconds. The full assessment covers 14 joints, bilateral and midline, and is designed to be done solo, without equipment, in your living room.
The ROM measurement portion takes 15–20 minutes. Entering your data into the submission form takes about 5 minutes. You only need to do it once every 6 weeks, each reassessment shows your progress and unlocks updated technique ratings.
Your Position Readiness Profile™ is your personalized technique map. Every one of the 124 techniques is rated GREEN (body-ready now), YELLOW (build toward it), or RED (avoid until ROM improves) based on your actual joint measurements compared to what that technique demands. It’s generated automatically and emailed to you the moment your assessment is processed.
Your personalized Position Readiness Profile™ is generated and sent to your inbox automatically, usually within minutes of your submission processing. You’ll also receive your dashboard access code so you can log in and explore My Body, My Game, and My Protocol right away.
Simple. When you log in to your dashboard for the first time, you will be taken to a secure payment window. Pay there and you are immediately returned to your dashboard with full access. No separate signup flow, no waiting. $149/year for athletes. If you have a promo code, you can apply it at that screen for your first year free.
After your assessment is processed, you’ll receive an email with your access code. Go to romrxbjj.com/dashboard, enter your email and code, and you’re in. No app download required.
You reassess. Six weeks of consistent mobility work moves the needle. Your updated ROM measurements generate a new Position Readiness Profile™, with more GREEN techniques unlocked and updated prescriptions for the joints still lagging. It’s a continuous improvement loop tied directly to your training.
