Asymmetry Is the #1 Predictor of Injury

Not weakness. Not overtraining. The difference between your left side and your right side.

A 35° asymmetry in shoulder flexion. A 9° gap in hip internal rotation. These aren't numbers most athletes know — but they determine whether De La Riva opens you up for a knee reap injury or whether your guard game stays intact.

ROMRxBJJ™ flags every asymmetry in your assessment, ranks them by injury risk, and prescribes exercises to close the gap.

Your protocol doesn't target weakness. It targets imbalance.
