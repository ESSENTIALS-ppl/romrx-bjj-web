2. Description of Service

ROMRxBJJ is a Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu performance and injury-prevention platform that provides users with Range of Motion (ROM) assessments, Position Readiness Scores (PRS), technique eligibility analysis, individualized movement protocols, training game plan generation, and AI-assisted coaching tools.

The Platform is designed to help practitioners understand their mobility limitations, reduce injury risk, and optimize their BJJ training. It is a fitness and educational tool, not a medical or clinical service.

5. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, belt rank, dominant side, and gym affiliation. This information is used to create and manage your account, personalize your experience, and enable coaching connections.

ROM Assessment Data

We collect the joint measurement data you enter during your ROM assessments, including measurements for hips, shoulders, ankles, lumbar spine, cervical spine, and thoracic spine. This data is used to generate your Position Readiness Score, technique eligibility analysis, and movement protocols.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the Platform, including pages visited, features used, sessions logged, protocols completed, and interactions with the AI coaching tool. This data is used to improve the Platform, personalize your experience, and generate aggregate analytics.

Device and Technical Data

We collect standard technical data including your IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system for security, performance, and analytics purposes.

Coach and Gym Data

If you are a coach or gym administrator, we collect your organization name, role, and the athletes you manage. This data is used to power coaching features and team analytics.
