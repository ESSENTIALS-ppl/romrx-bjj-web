Running an academy without body data is coaching blind.

Your athletes walk in with different ROM profiles, asymmetries, and injury histories across every coach and every mat. ROMRxBJJ for Schools gives you the full picture at the academy level.

📝
No Visibility Across Coaches
Head instructors can not see what individual coaches know about each athlete's body. Gaps get missed. Injuries happen that could have been prevented.
🏋
Curriculum Not Tied to Bodies
Curriculum is built on belt level, not body readiness. Athletes drill techniques their ROM cannot safely support, week after week.
📊
No School-Wide Analytics
You have no academy-wide picture of which ROM gaps are most common on your mats, or what is driving your injury rate across all students.
